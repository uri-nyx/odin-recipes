Pizza

Description

Pizzas are among the most well known italian dishes worldwide, both for its simplicity and exquisite taste. Here, you'll learn how to prepare a Margarita style pizza, a pizza that's fit for a Queen. You'll need:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Albahaca

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 200C
  2. Roll the dough
  3. Extend the tomato sauce
  4. Spread the Mozzarella evenly
  5. Bake for 10 to 15 minutes
